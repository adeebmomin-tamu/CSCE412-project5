Second Mile Mission Center

Second Mile Mission Center is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing essential services to under-resourced individuals and families in the Missouri City area. Their mission is to help those in crisis by offering food assistance, medical care, and educational support weekly. By creating a sense of community and judgement free atmospheres, Second Mile empowers individuals to overcome challenges and build a path toward a more stable and promising future. I often volunteer at Second Mile, and try to attend their drive-through food drives as often as possible.

iCERV/Ismaili Civic

iCERV (Ismaili Community Engaged in Responsible Volunteering) is a community service organization for members of the Ismaili community to engage in volunteer work. By organizing a wide range of service activities, from local community projects like food drives to even global work, iCERV allows individuals to volunteer, whether it be for social contribution or community service hours. Volunteers are encouraged to make a positive impact in areas such as education, health, environmental sustainability, and disaster relief. iCERVs goal is to build stronger, more compassionate communities through collective service, while promoting values of kindness, generosity, and unity, all of which are very important in the ismaili community.
I personally have volunteering with ICERV many times. From volunteering at the Houston Food Bank, to volunteering in local food drives in Sugar Land, and even participating in free blood testing, iCERV provides an endless amount of ways to volunteer.
